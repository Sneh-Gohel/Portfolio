S Messages

S Messages is an Android-based messaging app designed with simplicity and a smooth user experience in mind. It not only allows users to send and receive messages but also introduces a note-sharing feature that works like a status — enabling users to express "what's on their mind" just like Instagram or WhatsApp.

Users can also upload custom statuses, share ideas or thoughts as notes, and manage their online presence. The app includes profile customization options so each user can personalize their experience with ease.

Its clean UI and thoughtful features make it a modern and expressive platform for communication beyond just chatting.

VRS - Vehicle Rental System

VRS - Vehicle Rental System is a community-driven platform that allows users to rent vehicles remotely without needing to physically visit the rental location. It simplifies the process of browsing and comparing options, making it easier to find the best match based on user preferences.

With built-in QR code verification, the platform ensures secure and authentic user interactions. It empowers users to not only rent vehicles but also list their own vehicles for rental and apply as drivers, creating a full-circle ecosystem.

Inspired by platforms like OLX, VRS is made for the users and by the users, bringing flexibility, convenience, and a touch of personalization to the vehicle rental industry.

ArtVision AI

ArtVision AI is an image recognition system that can identify and classify artwork styles, artists, and historical periods. It uses deep learning to analyze visual patterns and provide contextual information about artworks.

The system achieves 92% accuracy on major art movements and can recognize over 200 famous artists' styles. It's particularly useful for art students, collectors, and museums for authentication and educational purposes.

What sets ArtVision apart is its explainability feature that highlights which visual elements contributed to its classification decisions.
